# Changelog

All notable changes to YGO: Nova are documented here.

## 1.0.2

### Added
- **Background music** — a looping main-menu track that mixes under the SFX,
  with an independent **music volume slider** in Audio settings (persisted).
- **Main-menu remake** — a cinematic dark scene: deep-black base, a breathing
  crimson "nova", rising embers, a rotating light sweep, a forged runic sigil,
  a hard vignette, and a subtle scatter of Egyptian hieroglyph marks.
- **Board-break puzzles** — go second against a preset modern boss board
  (Lunalight, Branded, Swordsoul, Mitsurugi), draw 6, and break it. A
  defensive AI fires the board's disruptions on your turn.
- Crisper card art via trilinear mipmaps.

### Changed / Fixed
- AI now takes a deliberate pause between every action (no more instant
  multi-plays), decoupled from the duel-speed setting.
- Phase transitions: end-of-turn skips straight to the End Phase; the coin-toss
  banner no longer gets swallowed by the Draw Phase; banners stay in sync with
  the engine.
- Right-click now means "No" on Yes/No prompts and "pass" on chain responses.
- Refreshed the red & black theme across the app.

## 1.0.1
- Versioning, in-app update check, and release packaging groundwork.

## 1.0.0
- First beta: offline duels vs. a heuristic AI, deck builder, replays,
  testing mode, online relay multiplayer (foundation), and LAN scaffolding.
